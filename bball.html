Stephen Curry

The man who changed basketball

Stephen Curry isn’t just a basketball player. His story is one of overcoming obstacles and defying expectations, and it’s why he has become my inspiration.

Stephen "Steph" Curry is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Many analysts and players have called him the greatest shooter in NBA history. He is credited with revolutionizing the game of basketball by inspiring teams to regularly utilize the three-point shot. A seven-time NBA All-Star, Curry has been named the NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) twice and has won three NBA championships with the Warriors. Stephen Curry of the Golden State Warriors was the first person to be named Most Valuable Player by unanimous vote in NBA history.
